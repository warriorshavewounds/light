My Encounter With Angels and of bigamy.

Before I turned 10 years I had angelic encounters. Of one I was told of the events of the current pandemic, even the wearing of masks. But I would touch on the other encounter.
On this occasion appearing in the form of a man, the angel spoke over me and explained that some people were going to try to harm me, but I should be rest assured that they cannot touch me.
Not everyone had those rare encounters that put a hedge around them in the beginning. I am sharing this today, but some people who became victims of enchantments, spells and foundation demands have not survived to be able to tell their stories.
It is not great that a tongue speaking, miracle working, vision seeing christian would fall victim to ungodly family pattens and unsolicited suffering.

I hope to in this series emphasise on

  • Character
  • Exposure
  • and crucially the matter of deliverance

    • because a christian without a clean break from their ancient traditional past is practicing bigamy...


Today's short write up is an introduction to the sequel.
